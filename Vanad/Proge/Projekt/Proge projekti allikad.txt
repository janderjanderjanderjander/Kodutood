1. Peab paluma seda:
Open Thonny:

Launch the Thonny IDE on your computer.
Open the Package Manager:

In Thonny, go to the "Tools" menu and select "Manage Packages..."
Search for Pygame:

In the "Package Manager" window, there is a search bar. Enter "pygame" into the search bar.
Install Pygame:

Once you see "pygame" in the search results, select it, and click the "Install" button.
Wait for Installation:

Thonny will download and install Pygame and its dependencies. You'll see progress in the lower part of the window.
Close Package Manager:

Once the installation is complete, you can close the "Package Manager" window.
Verify the Installation:

Open Thonny's shell or a new script, and try importing Pygame:
